A Tradition of Excellence

Established in 1956, Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) has grown into a premier institution of higher learning. We are committed to producing professionals who are not only academically competent but also ethically sound and socially responsible.

With a focus on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) alongside Humanities and Social Sciences, UiTM offers a comprehensive educational experience that prepares students for the challenges of the modern world.
