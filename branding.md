# Branding Guidelines

## Brand Identity
**Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM)**

The branding reflects a professional, academic, and modern identity, characterized by the authoritative use of purple and gold.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
*   **UiTM Purple (Official Corporate)**: `#753895`
    *   *Web Implementation (Buttons/Actions)*: `#3F51B5` (Deep Indigo) / `#7164B5` (Lighter Purple)
    *   Used for: Primary buttons, headers, brand accents.
*   **UiTM Gold (Accent)**: `#F1C40F`
    *   Used for: Highlights, "Apply Now" calls-to-action, logo elements.

### Secondary Colors
*   **Midnight Blue**: `#20356B` or `#012e6f`
    *   Used for: Structural elements, footers, deep contrast.
*   **Backgrounds**:
    *   White: `#FFFFFF`
    *   Light Gray: `#F9F9F9`

## Typography

### Primary Typeface
**Poppins** (Sans-serif)
*   Usage: Body text, navigation, and primary headings.
*   Characteristics: Modern, geometric, friendly, and legible.

### Secondary Typeface
**Tahoma** (Sans-serif)
*   Usage: Selected sub-headings (e.g., H4).

### Fallbacks
*   Roboto (often cited in corporate guides)
*   System sans-serif

## Visual Style
*   **Clean & Professional**: Generous whitespace with light gray backgrounds.
*   **Modern Web Elements**: Rounded buttons, clear calls-to-action in primary colors.
*   **Imagery**: Utilization of high-quality campus or academic photography.
